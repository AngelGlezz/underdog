About Us
Patricio Villalobos Cuevas and Miguel Ramírez Lombana, or simply Pato & Mike. We have been serial entrepreneurs since 1999 and partners for +23 years. We created underdog as our "personal trademark" for all projects we launch. It represents our core values, our way of working, our teamwork vision and the legacy we want to leave behind.
underdog
/ˈəndərˌdôɡ/

noun
An underdog is a person or group in a competition, usually in sports and creative works, who is largely expected to lose. The party, team, or individual expected to win is called the favorite or top dog. In the case where an underdog wins, the outcome is an upset.
Mike
Serial entrepreneur who started his first company in 1998.  In 1999, “Mike” co-founded mediotiempo.com, a pioneer project on the Mexican Internet landscape, which eventually became the leading sports website, acquired in 2010 by Time Warner. After the acquisition, Mike headed Expansion's Interactive division.

In 2013, Mike co-founded Soccerly, a soccer digital platform for the U.S. market and in 2014, launched a portfolio of social-first publishing brands @juanfutbol, @fridapop and @juanbeisbol. These assets were acquired by Playmaker Capital in 2022.

In 2016, co-founded Tikitaka, that today is a leading sports marketing agency that serves leagues, events, clubs, sponsors and startups. In 2018 Sondermut was created to provide consulting services to empower innovation through technology services. 

Mike has served in the advisory and board of directors of several companies. He has an MBA from Kellogg School of Management, currently lives in Mexico City, is married and a proud father of three.
Pato
Mexican entrepreneur with a unique passion for technology, media and football. Married, father of four kids and proud of being an Underdog.

Avid fan of Lio Messi and the perfection of FC Barcelona. Admirer of Olafur Eliasson, Ferran Adrià and Yoshitomo Nara.

In 1999 he co-founded mediotiempo.com as a hobby and eventually turned it into one of the biggest online properties in Latin America and number one sports site in Mexico and a digital-media success story as it was acquired in 2010 by Grupo Expansión, a Time Warner Company at that time.

After serving four years as Expansion's CTO, in 2012 along with Mike, he continued the entreprenurial path with the main goal of building platforms that embrace the digital transformation of sports and other industries.

In 2018 he co-founded Sondermut, to serve SMEs with better and smoother technology to enable the world ot tomorrow.
